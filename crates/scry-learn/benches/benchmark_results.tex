{\footnotesize\color{neutralgray}
\textbf{Methodology:}
Real UCI CSVs (iris 150$\times$4, wine 178$\times$13, breast\_cancer 569$\times$30).
Single thread: Rust rayon=1, Python n\_jobs=1 + OMP/MKL/OPENBLAS\_NUM\_THREADS=1.
Memory: counting allocator (Rust), tracemalloc (Python).
Timing: Criterion (Rust), median of 30 runs (Python).
Matched hyperparameters: max\_depth=10, n\_trees=20, k=5, max\_iter=200.
KNN ``training'' is lazy (store-only) --- not benchmarked in Rust; sklearn times shown for reference.
